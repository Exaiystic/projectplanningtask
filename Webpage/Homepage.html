Welcome!

Laughton-en-le-Morthen Parish Council serves the village of Laughton-en-le-Morthen along with the hamlets of Brookhouse, Slade Hooton, Carr and Newhall. Laughton-en-le-Morthen is a small dormitory village and civil parish in the metropolitan borough or Rotherham, lying to the south of Rotherham, South Yorkshire.
